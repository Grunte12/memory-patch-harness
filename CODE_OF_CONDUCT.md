# Code of Conduct

This project is small, technical, and experimental. The community standard is simple:

- be respectful,
- assume good intent,
- criticize ideas and evidence rather than people,
- disclose uncertainty,
- do not pressure maintainers for unpaid urgency,
- do not post private data or secrets,
- accept that benchmark claims need reproducible evidence.

Unacceptable behavior includes harassment, personal attacks, discriminatory language, doxxing, spam, and knowingly submitting private or unsafe data.

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove comments and issues that violate these rules. Repeated or severe violations may result in blocking from the project.
